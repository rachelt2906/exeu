Our Services

Safety & Compliance

EX Europe Electrical provides specialist electrical safety and compliance testing for businesses across the UK.

01 / 03
Portable Appliance Test (PAT)

Comprehensive testing of portable electrical appliances to ensure they are safe for workplace use. Covers earth bond, insulation resistance, and leakage — with certified pass/fail documentation for every item.

02 / 03
Microwave Emission Test

Specialist testing of microwave ovens measuring radiation leakage levels. Ensures appliances comply with the legal 5 mW/cm² threshold, protecting staff and meeting Health & Safety obligations.

03 / 03
WEEE Disposal

Compliant disposal of Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment under the WEEE Directive. Safe, documented, and environmentally responsible collection and recycling of end-of-life electrical assets.
